Basic

  • $\underbrace{F^{\prime}(x)}_{\text{primitive}} = f(x),\displaystyle{\int \underbrace{f(x)\mathrm{d}x}_{\text{differential form}}=F(x)+C}$.
  • Nonelementary: $e^{-x^2}, \dfrac{e^x}{x}, \dfrac{1}{\ln x},\sin{x^2}, \cos{x^2}, \dfrac{\sin x}{x}, \dfrac{\cos x}{x}$.
  • $\dfrac{1}{x} \to \ln{|x|}, x^{\alpha} \to \dfrac{x^{\alpha+1}}{\alpha+1}, a^x \to a^x\ln a$.
  • $\sin x \to -\cos x, \cos x \to \sin x$.
  • $\tan x \to -\ln{|\cos x|}, \cot x \to \ln{|\sin x|}$.
  • $\sec x \to \ln{|\sec x+\tan x|}, \csc x \to \ln{|\csc x-\cot x|}$.
  • $\arcsin x \to x\arcsin x+\sqrt{1-x^2}$.
  • $\arccos x \to x\arccos x-\sqrt{1-x^2}$.
  • $\arctan x \to x\arctan x-\dfrac{1}{2}\ln{(1+x^2)}$.

Square

  • $\dfrac{1}{\sqrt{a^2-x^2}} \to \arcsin{\dfrac{x}{a}}, \dfrac{1}{\sqrt{x^2\pm a^2}} \to \ln{|x+\sqrt{x^2\pm a^2}|}$.
  • $\dfrac{1}{a^2+x^2} \to \arctan{\dfrac{x}{a}}, \dfrac{1}{a^2-x^2} \to \dfrac{1}{2a}\ln{\left\vert\dfrac{a+x}{a-x}\right\vert}$.
  • $\sqrt{a^2+x^2} \to \dfrac{x}{2}\sqrt{a^2+x^2}+\dfrac{a^2}{2}\ln{|x+\sqrt{x^2+a^2}|}$.
  • $\sqrt{a^2-x^2} \to \dfrac{x}{2}\sqrt{a^2-x^2}+\dfrac{a^2}{2}\arcsin\dfrac{x}{a}$.

Calculate method

  • It is linear.
  • $\displaystyle{\int{u(x)v^{\prime}(x)\mathrm{d}x}=\int{u(x)v(x)\mathrm{d}x}-\int{u^{\prime}(x)v(x)}\mathrm{d}x}$.
  • $F(x)=\int{f(u)\mathrm{d}u}+C$ on an interval $I_u$, $u(x):I_x \to I_u \in C^1(I_x)$, then $\int{f(u(x))u^{\prime}(x)\mathrm{d}x}=F(u(x))+C$.
  • In addtional, $u^{\prime}(x) \ne 0$, it can be used inversely.

Change of Variable

  • $\displaystyle{\int{\dfrac{\mathrm{d}x}{{(x+a)}^m{(x+b)}^n}}}, t=\dfrac{x+a}{x+b}$.
  • $y= \sqrt[n]{\dfrac{ax+b}{cx+d}}=t$.
  • $y=\sqrt{x^2+1}=tx+1 \text{ or }tx-1 \text{ or }x-t$.
  • $y=\sqrt{x^2-1}=t(x-1) \text{ or }t(x+1) \text{ or }x-t$.
  • $y=\sqrt{1-x^2}=t(1-x) \text{ or }t(1+x) \text{ or }tx \pm 1$.
  • $y=\sqrt{ax^2+bx+c}=\pm\sqrt{a}x+t, a>0$.
  • $y=\sqrt{ax^2+bx+c}=tx\pm\sqrt{c}, c>0$.
  • $y=\sqrt{a(x-x_1)(x-x_2)}=t(x-x_1)$.

Rational Fraction

  • $\dfrac{P(x)}{Q(x)}=p(x)+\sum\limits_{j=1}^{l}\left( \sum\limits_{k=1}^{k_j}\dfrac{a_{jk}}{(x-x_j)^k} \right)+\sum\limits_{j=1}^{n}\left( \sum\limits_{k=1}^{m_j}\dfrac{b_{jk}x+c_{jk}}{(x^2+p_jx+q_j)^k} \right)$
  • $\displaystyle{\int\dfrac{\left(Ax+B\right)\mathrm{d}x}{x^2+px+q}=\dfrac{A}{2}\ln{\left(x^2+px+q\right)}}+\dfrac{2B-Ap}{\sqrt{4q-p^2}}\arctan\dfrac{2x+p}{\sqrt{4q-p^2}}+C$
  • Ostrogradsky's integration method: $\displaystyle{\int {R(x)\over P(x)}\mathrm{d}x = {T(x)\over S(x)} + \int {X(x)\over Y(x)}\mathrm{d}x}$, $S(x)$ is the greatest common divisor of $P(x)$ and $P^{\prime}(x)$, $P(x)=S(x)Y(x)$.

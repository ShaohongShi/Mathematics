Product Groups

   $H,K \le G,f:H \times K \to G, (h,k) \mapsto hk$
  • $f$ is injective $\Leftrightarrow H \cap K=\{1\}$.
  • $f$ is a homomorphism $\Leftrightarrow hk=kh,\forall h \in H,k \in K$.
  • $H \trianglelefteq G \Rightarrow HK \le G$
  • $f$ is an isomorphism iff $H \cap K=\{1\}$, $HK=G$ and $H,K \trianglelefteq G$.

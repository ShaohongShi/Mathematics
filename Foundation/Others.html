Trigonometry

  • Euler Formula
    • $$e^{iz}=\cos z+i \sin z \, .$$ $$\sin z= \sin x\cosh y+i \cos x \sinh y \, .$$ $$\cos z =\cos x \cosh y-i\sin x\sinh y \, .$$
  • Basic
    • $$\sin^2 x+\cos^2 x=\sec^2 x-\tan^2 x=\csc^2 x- \cot^2 x=1 \, .$$ $$\sin(-x)=-\sin x, \cos (-x)=\cos x, \tan (-x)=-\tan x \, .$$
  • Sum and Minus
    • $$\sin (x \pm y)=\sin x\cos y \pm \cos x \sin y \, .$$ $$\cos (x \pm y)=\cos x \cos y \mp \sin x \sin y \, .$$ $$\tan (x \pm y)=\dfrac{\tan x \pm \tan y}{1 \mp \tan x \tan y} \, .$$
  • Multiply
    • $$\sin 3x = 3\sin x-4 \sin ^3 x, \cos 3x=4\cos^3 x-3\cos x \, .$$ $$\sin^2 x = \dfrac{1-\cos 2x}{2}, \cos^2 x=\dfrac{1+\cos 2x}{2} \, .$$ $$\tan x = \dfrac{\sin 2x}{1 + \cos 2x}=\dfrac{1 - \cos 2x}{\sin 2x} \, .$$ $$\sin x=\dfrac{2t}{1+t^2}\, , \cos x=\dfrac{1-t^2}{1+t^2}\, ,$$ $$\mathrm{d}x=\dfrac{2\mathrm{d}t}{1+t^2}\, , t=\tan \dfrac{x}{2} \, .$$
  • Sum to Product
    • $$\sin x +\sin y= 2 \sin \dfrac{x+y}{2} \cos \dfrac{x-y}{2} \, .$$ $$\sin x -\sin y= 2 \cos \dfrac{x+y}{2} \sin \dfrac{x-y}{2} \, .$$ $$\cos x +\cos y= 2 \cos \dfrac{x+y}{2} \cos \dfrac{x-y}{2} \, .$$ $$\cos x -\cos y= -2 \sin \dfrac{x+y}{2} \sin \dfrac{x-y}{2} \, .$$
  • Series
    • $$\sum\limits_{k=1}^{n}\sin k=\dfrac{1-\cos (n+1)}{2 \sin 1} \, .$$ $$\prod\limits_{k=1}^{n}\cos \dfrac{x}{2^k}=\dfrac{\sin x}{2^n \sin \dfrac{x}{2^n}} \, .$$ $$\sum\limits_{k=1}^{n}\dfrac{1}{2^k}\tan \dfrac{x}{2^k}=\dfrac{1}{2^n}\cot \dfrac{x}{2^n}-\cot x \, .$$
